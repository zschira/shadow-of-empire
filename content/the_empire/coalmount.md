The predominantly Gnomish and Dwarven kingdom of Coalmount has long been
one of the most insular and indepent kingdoms of The Empire. Nearly all 
residents live in the mountain itself, and the surrounding deserts are
made impassable by The Cursed Sands. The only known way in or out is
through ancient volcanic tunnels which run under the sands, and are
closely controlled by Coalmount.
