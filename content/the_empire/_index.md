## The Five Kingdoms
At the end of the Age of Gods, The Empire was partitioned into 5 semi-independent
kingdoms, but with ultimate power lying in The Emporer who would rule out of 
Centerra. However, in the 1,000 years since its establishment, The Empire's control
over the continent has weakened.
