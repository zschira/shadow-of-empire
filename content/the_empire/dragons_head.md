Dragon's Head lies on the large island south of the main continent.
Its isolation and frigid climate has made Dragon's Head a lightly
populated and underdeveloped kingdom compared to those of the main
continent. Recent years have brought instability and chaos to the
kingdom. The King barely clings to power as warlords capture territory,
and monsters openly roam the countryside. In the chaos, The Empire has
recently pulled most of its forces off the island, leaving the King's
position even more precarious.

## Important Places
**Ironscale** - the capitol city where the king attempts to maintain 
control. Despite being by far the biggest city on Dragon's Head, it's
still small in comparison to the cities of the main continent. Its
population has also further declined as the kingdom's economy and trade
have suffered, leaving entire blocks completely abandoned.
