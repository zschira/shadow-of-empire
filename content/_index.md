## The Second Empire of Abadar
![World map](/img/the_second_empire.png)

### Introduction
Welcome adventurer to The Second Empire of Abadar!

You find yourself in a world of ancient gods, magical beasts, and
political intrigue. The Empire draws its lineage back to a time when
the gods wandered the mortal realm.

Fearing for the mortals in a world of gods, the god Abadar created the
continent of Centerra, and established The First Empire as a refuge
for mortals. This empire existed in peace for millenia before the
arrival of the dark god Asmodeus, who brought chaos and suffering to
the realm. A terrible war was fought to stop to Asmodeus, but
even after decades of brutal fighting, victory remained elusive.

Finally, Abadar and a group of other gods divised a plan to construct
several powerful artifacts that could be used to seal the gods out of
the mortal realm altogether. And so, the war was brought to an end.
Five new kingdoms were established, each to protect one of these
artifacts. Together these kingdoms became The Second Empire who's
seat of power lies in the most powerful kingdom named after the 
very continent, Centerra.

... or so the story goes.

There are other religions in this world with their own stories to explain
the world and the gods, but those which explicitly contradict this story
are ... *frowned upon*.

You find yourself in the least populated "backwater" kingdom of The Empire,
Dragon's Head. This kingdom lies on an island of its own south of the main
continent, and finds itself in a period of upheavel. The King barely clings
to power as competing warlords seize territory, the roads have become
overrun with banditry, and terrifying beasts, once only found in remote
wilderness, now openly attack towns and travellers. The Empire has pulled
back its troops leaving the King with only a small regiment to hold the
capitol city of Ironscale.
